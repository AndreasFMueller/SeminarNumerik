  $$ \frac {\partial u}{\partial t}+u{\frac {\partial u}{\partial x}}=0 $$

  $$ u_{i}^{n} = \frac{\Delta{x}\, u^{n-1}_{i}\,}{- \Delta{t}\, u^{n-1}_{i-1}\, + \Delta{t}\, u^{n-1}_{i}\, + \Delta{x}\,}$$
